Fruit and Cheese Board

Ingredients

  1. 10 fresh strawberries, halved
  2. 8 fresh or dried figs, halved
  3. 2 small navel oranges, thinly sliced
  4. 12 ounces seedless red grapes (about 1-1/2 cups)
  5. 1 medium mango, halved and scored
  6. 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  7. 1 cup fresh blackberries
  8. 1/2 cup dried banana chips
  9. 2 large kiwifruit, peeled, halved and thinly sliced
  10. 12 ounces seedless watermelon (about 6 slices)
  11. 1/2 cup unblanched almonds
  12. 8 ounces Brie cheese
  13. 8 ounces mascarpone cheese
  14. 1/2 cup honey

Directions

  1. On a large platter or cutting board, arrange fruit, almonds and cheeses. Place honey in a small jar; tuck jar among fruit.
